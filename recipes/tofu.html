Pan-Fried Tofu

Description

Pan-Fried Tofu is a simple yet flavorful dish made with crispy tofu cubes marinated in a savory blend of soy sauce, garlic, and spices. Perfect as a protein-packed main or side dish, it pairs beautifully with rice, noodles, or vegetables.

Ingredients
  • Firm tofu (1 block, drained)
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Ginger (grated)
  • Cornstarch
  • Sesame oil
  • Vegetable oil
  • Green onions (optional, for garnish)
  • Red chili flakes (optional, for spice)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps
  1. Press the tofu block to remove excess water and cut it into bite-sized cubes.
  2. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, minced garlic, grated ginger, and a pinch of black pepper to make the marinade.
  3. Coat the tofu cubes in the marinade and let them sit for 15–20 minutes.
  4. Sprinkle cornstarch over the tofu and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Heat vegetable oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  6. Place the tofu cubes in the skillet and cook until golden brown and crispy on all sides, about 3–5 minutes per side.
  7. Drizzle with sesame oil for added flavor and toss gently.
  8. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped green onions and red chili flakes, if desired.
  9. Serve hot as a main dish or side dish with your favorite accompaniments.

Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a hearty Italian classic made with layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey melted cheese.
Perfectly baked to golden perfection, this dish is comforting, flavorful, and ideal for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Fresh parsley

Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet, sauté onion and garlic until softened.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned; drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper; simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and half the parmesan cheese.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and shredded mozzarella. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
  9. Top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  12. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
